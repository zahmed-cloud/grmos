FAQ

Questions we get asked

Is GRM a real product?↓
Yes. Though we strongly recommend using it responsibly. All data is stored in your browser — nobody else can see your pipeline.
Is this encouraging bad behaviour?↓
No. GRM simply provides operational clarity. What you do with that clarity is entirely your responsibility. We are not your therapist.
Can I use GRM for just one relationship?↓
Absolutely. The Free plan was built for exactly that. Some of our most loyal users manage exactly one stakeholder and are very happy about it.
Is my data private?↓
All relationship data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We don't have a server. Nobody is reading your notes. We promise.
What if someone discovers my dashboard?↓
We recommend Panic Mode. Navigate to the dashboard, click the red button, and let our apology generator handle the rest. We've prepared for this scenario.
